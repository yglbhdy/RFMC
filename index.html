Open Source

Third-Party firmware is welcome, especially OpenWrt. We will push our source on github, oschina. In the meanwhile, pull requests and feedbacks are appreciated.

Complete BSP Support

The R&D team has customized a system called RosyWrt in depth based on OpenWrt, and we are committed to developing lightweight, experience-friendly, secure and easy-to-use embedded systems that allow devices to work more reliably.

Free Applications

We developed a new QoS plug-in based on nftables. Main features: static QoS, dynamic QoS, and traffic shaping are supported as long as you are running 4.2 or above kernel.

Customization
