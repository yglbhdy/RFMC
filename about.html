Who we are

ROSINSON is a company that engaged in embedded software & hardware development. The core team is made up of a group of developers who loved OpenWrt and Linux, they are young, have ideas, and actively involved in community contributions, especially the OpenWrt. We aim to create communities for routers, nas, mini-pc and any other popular hardware products. We are willing to interact with people with same interests and develop popular products together. Currently, RosyWrt is one of the brands owned by ROSINSON.
